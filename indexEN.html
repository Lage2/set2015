What is our vision?

This event aims to promote the exchange of experiences and ideas related to the field of IT, as well as its influence in the corporate world. Being organized by students from Instituto Superior Técnico (campus do Taguspark), this event has had a growing participation by people from this area, already being a renowned event for the presentation of innovative projects and with a strong market impact.

What makes us unique?

SET - Semana Empresarial e Tecnológica is an event organized by students, for students of IST, so we know their interests and needs. As such, the result is an event with extremely interesting lectures and workshops which close the gap between the corporate and academic worlds.

How to get here

By Train
By Bus
By Taxi
By Car

There are connections from the Lisboa-Sintra and Lisboa-Cascais Train lines. The company that manages the public transportation for this area is Vimeca Transportes.

The bus company Vimeca has a few routes that pass by the campus at Taguspark. For detailed and up-to-date information please visit their website.

For Students and Employees at Técnico Lisboa, Shuttle IST, connects to the campus at Taguspark, free of charge between 07:00 and 20:10.

Taxis allow access to Taguspark from anywhere in the city, however the cost depends on the distance, traffic and the duration of the trip. A trip from downtown Lisbon to Taguspark as an average duration of 30 minutes.

Using the A5 Lisbon-Cascais Highway: Exit at Cacém/Porto Salvo. The freeway between the A5 and Cacém allows more direct access to the campus.

Using IC 19 - Exit at Cacém, in the direction of Paço d'Arcos.
