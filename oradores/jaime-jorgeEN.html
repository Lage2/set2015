Jaime Jorge

CEO and Co-Founder of Codacy

Jaime Jorge is the CEO and co-founder of Codacy, an automated code-revision service, which allows teams and companies to have a more efficient and less frustrating development process.

Jaime Jorge was trained in Software Engineering, area in which he holds a Master's Degree, acquired in Instituto Superior Técnico.

Jaime's personal acheivements include:

  • Creating a London-Based Company as soon as he was out of college.
  • Hireing the best people he knows, so they can be a part of his vision
  • Managing to earn $500,000
  • Making Codacy grow, so that it currently has 3000 collaborators all around the world.

Acoording to Jaime Jorge his company, Codacy, has allowed him to develop several new skills in the areas of: fundraising, recruiting, software engineering, growth hacking, public relations and product development and sales.
