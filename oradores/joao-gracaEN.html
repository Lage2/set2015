João Graça

Founder and CTO Unbabel, Inc

João Graça is currently the CTO of Unbabel. He previously was the Natural Language Processing expert and data scientist at Dezine and Flashgroud.

João got his Doctorate Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning at Instituto Superior Técnico, together with the University of Pennsylvania with the professors Fernando Pereira, Ben Taskar and Luísa Coheur. Author of various articles in the area, his primary research topics are Machine Learning with lateral information, unsupervised learning and machine translation. João is also one of the Lisbon Machine Learning Summer School Co-Founders.

Unbabel is a human quality translation service. By combining Machine with Human Translation we capture the best of both worlds: the speed and scale of machine and crowd translation, key for the quick turnaround and large content volumes, and the Human Quality, indispensable for communicating with customers. And the cherry on top is our affordable price. Working with customers like Coursera, Yummly and Pinterest. Unbabel is backed by Google Ventures, Matrix Partners, Y Combinator and others. The company was founded in August 2013 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California with offices in Lisbon, Portugal.
