Miguel Gonçalves

Founder of Spark Agency

Miguel Gonçalves is a creative individual with plenty of dreams and a child-like imagination and he believes that ideas come to fruition with the passion and envolvement of great teams. He founded Spark Agency, a creative agency specialized in corporate culture and behaviour development.

He also worked on countless projects such as:

Pitch Bootcamp – becoming Europe’s leading skills accelerator

So You Think You Can Pitch – An event that joins together a new generation of companies with young people who want to work.

Udini – A mentorship network that brings CEOs and Businessmen closer to people who want to start a new business.

Looop – Corporate Internal Communication Mobile App

Cloudrh – Talent management platform

Arpera – Geo-referenced messaging mobile app

Zappeo – A mobile app that empowers networking at events

At the moment, Miguel Gonçalves is working with over 200 of the most interesting and competitive firms in Portugal, such as: Montepio, Vodafone, PT, Pfizer, BMW, EDP, Bial, Optimus, Santander, Fidelidade, AXA e Pfizer, among others.
