Paulo Rosado

Founder and CEO of OutSystems

Paulo Rosado is the founder and CEO of Outsystems. Since the company's foundation in 2001, he has taken it from start-up to an international leader position in cutting-edge technology through innovative aproaches for the distributions of corporate applications that use web and mobile support. As CEO he is responsible for the day-to-day operations as well as leading product development and the companies technological strategy.

Before OutSystems, Paulo was the executive vice-president of Global Marketing at Altitude Software, a CRM company with business in 32 countries. Before joining Altitude, he was a co-founder of Intervento, an e-business software infrastructe company, where he was the CEO until the company's sale in 1999. Previously, Paulo worked at Silicon Valley for Oracle Corp, where he occupied several positions in the R&D and product management departments. Paulo has participated in several executive education programs and holds a Master's Degree in Computational Science through Stanford University and he graduated in Computational Engineering through Universidade Nova de Lisboa.
